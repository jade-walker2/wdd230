Our Story

Bountiful Foods was created in the backyard of John Smith Sr. John lived in Carlsbad and had one of the most prestine gardens in the valley. John had a love for his community and wanted to give them the best produce in the whole city. It started out by going to farmers markets and selling produce, to then being in small locally owned grocery stores, to what it is today. We value John's effort, and want to continue his legacy of delivering the best produce to the city of Carlsbad and the coastal area of Southern California.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the good people of Carlsbad with the freshest produce at the most affordable cost. We work hard to keep things fresh and we take pride in our work. We have grown now to not only serve the good people of Carlsbad, but we now are able to provide produce to all Southern California with our Food Truck Delivery System. We will not stop until every customer is 100% satisfied with our product.
