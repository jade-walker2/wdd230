Carlsbad is a coastal city in southern Orange County, California, United States. Carlsbad is known for swimming and sandy beaches. The City of Carlsbad is located in the coastal center of Orange County, with Los Angeles County to the north and San Diego County to the south. It has an estimated permanent population of 86,738 but during the summer months, the population grows to more than 100,000 with 20,000 to 100,000 tourists daily. Carlsbad is known for its fine residential areas, modern shopping facilities, strong business community and quality school system. It surrounds Carlsbad Bay where approximately 4,300 boats of all types are docked within the 21-square-mile harbor area.
