Site Purpose

The purpose of Bountiful Foods website is to allow customers to know where they can come to get the best home-grown fruit and produce. Bountiful Foods is a store located in Carlsbad California. They have alot of walk in customers, but they also have online orders. This website will be what connects the online customers and the product. The goal of this website is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas. This website will have a page that allows the user to make their own fruit drink with up to 3 ingredients and special instructions. This website will be the customers one stop shop to eating healthy.

Target Audience

The target audience for this website will obviously be those who love eating healthy and want to have the freshest of food. These are also people who love to support locally grown produce. They are people who take pride in what they put inside their bodies and want something different than your typical greens smoothie. They are fitness influencers, health specialists, heath promoting organizations such as gyms, schools, and hospitals. Lastly, this website will target just the general health seeking people.
